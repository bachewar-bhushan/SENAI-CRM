# Refund Policy

## 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Customers on Standard or Professional plans can request a full refund within **14 calendar days** of purchase if they haven't integrated more than 1,000 emails.

- Request must come from account owner
- Must include reason for refund
- Processing takes 5-10 business days

## No Refunds After 14 Days

After the 14-day window:
- Prepaid service cannot be refunded
- Unused balance cannot be transferred
- Service can be downgraded for future billing periods

**Exception**: See "Special Circumstances" below.

## Credit vs Refund

Customers may request **service credits** instead of refunds:
- Credits remain on account for 12 months
- Can be applied to any future invoice
- Full credit value awarded (no penalties)
- Credits recommended for customers who may return

## Special Circumstances

### Cancellation Requests (Churn Retention)

**Offer 1: Service Credit**
- Offer 1-3 months of free service credit
- Keeps customer on platform

**Offer 2: Plan Downgrade**
- Downgrade to Starter plan at 50% discount for 3 months
- No penalty, can upgrade back anytime

**Offer 3: Custom Deal**
- For high-value customers: negotiate custom pricing
- Waive setup fees, provide implementation credits
- Escalate to VP Sales for approval

### Billing Errors or Service Issues

If customer requests refund due to:
- Duplicate charges: Full refund + apology
- Service outage >24 hours: 1 month free credit + RCA report
- Data loss: Full refund + Data Recovery Services credit (,000 value)

## Enterprise Contract Refunds

- Governed by individual contract terms
- Typically: No refunds for pre-paid periods
- Credits applied if service terms breached
- 30-day notice required for cancellation

## Subscription Auto-Renewal

**Cancellation**: Cancel anytime via dashboard
- No penalty or hidden fees
- Service stops at end of billing period
- Pro-rated refund if cancelled mid-cycle

**Renewal Logic**:
- Card on file charged automatically
- Billing date: Same day each month
- Failed payment = 2 retry attempts, then suspension
