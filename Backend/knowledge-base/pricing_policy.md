# Pricing Policy

## Subscription Tiers

### Standard Plan
- **Cost**: $299/month
- **Seats**: Up to 10 users
- **Features**: Basic CRM, email management, 1 custom integration, 24h support response
- **API Rate Limit**: 1,000 requests/minute
- **Data Storage**: 50 GB

### Professional Plan
- **Cost**: $799/month
- **Seats**: Up to 50 users
- **Features**: Advanced analytics, custom workflows, 5 integrations, 4h support response
- **API Rate Limit**: 5,000 requests/minute
- **Data Storage**: 500 GB
- **Training**: 2 hours included per month

### Enterprise Plan
- **Cost**: Custom pricing (typically $2,000+/month)
- **Seats**: Unlimited
- **Features**: Full customization, dedicated support, 24/7 on-call SRE, unlimited integrations
- **API Rate Limit**: Custom (often 10,000+ req/min)
- **Data Storage**: Unlimited
- **Training**: Unlimited
- **SLA**: 99.99% uptime guarantee with credits

## Special Discounts

### Non-Profit Discount
- **Eligibility**: Registered 501(c)(3) in the US or equivalent tax-exempt status
- **Discount**: 30% off Standard or Professional plan (recurring)
- **Duration**: Indefinite as long as non-profit status maintained
- **Documentation Required**: Copy of tax exemption letter

### Academic/Educational Discount
- **Eligibility**: Universities, colleges, research institutions
- **Discount**: 50% off Standard plan for up to 200 concurrent users per semester
- **Documentation Required**: .edu email or letter from institution

### Volume Discount
- **10-25 seats**: 10% off listed price
- **26-50 seats**: 15% off listed price
- **50+ seats**: Contact sales for custom pricing

## Pro-Rata Billing

When upgrading or downgrading mid-cycle:
- **Upgrade**: Charge difference for remaining days at higher plan rate (prorated)
- **Downgrade**: Credit applied to next billing cycle
- **Refund Window**: Credits can be applied to future invoices; no refund to payment method
- **Calculation**: Daily rate = (monthly price / 30 days) × remaining days

**Example**: Customer on Standard ($299) upgrades to Professional ($799) on day 10 of 30-day cycle.
- Days remaining: 20
- Upgrade cost: (($799 - $299) / 30) × 20 = $333.33
- Charge to card: $333.33

## Enterprise Custom Pricing

For deals exceeding:
- 100+ concurrent users
- Custom SLA requirements (99.99%+ uptime)
- Data residency in specific country
- Dedicated infrastructure

**Process**:
1. Qualify with sales team (contact sales@senai.io)
2. Provide requirements & use case
3. 5-10 business day custom proposal
4. Legal review & negotiation
5. Contract execution

## Payment Terms

- **Standard**: Monthly, auto-renew (payment method on file)
- **Enterprise**: Net 30 invoice (quarterly or annual options available)
- **Accepted Methods**: Credit card, ACH, wire transfer
- **Failed Payment**: After 2 failed attempts, account suspended until payment renewed

## Cancellation

- **Standard Plans**: Cancel anytime, account closes at end of billing period
- **Enterprise Contracts**: 30-day written notice required per contract terms
- **No Refunds**: Prepaid service cannot be refunded; credits may apply to future invoices
